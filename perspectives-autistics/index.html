Perspectives from Autistic Individuals

Resources offering insights and perspectives from autistic individuals about their experiences and views on autism.

This collection of resources offers insights and perspectives from autistic individuals about their experiences and views on autism. These resources cover various topics, including self-advocacy, neurodiversity, challenges faced by autistic individuals, and practical advice for navigating life on the autism spectrum. By sharing their experiences, autistic individuals provide valuable support and guidance to others in the community.
