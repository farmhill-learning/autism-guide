Employment & Adulthood

Resources covering a comprehensive range of topics related to work, adulting, and independent living for autistic individuals.

1 Resource

Navigating the world of work and adulting can present unique challenges and opportunities for autistic individuals. This collection of resources is designed to provide valuable insights and practical strategies for autistic adults, their families, employers, and support networks to foster successful employment and independent living.

Being an Adult Autistic

This resource explores the transition to adulthood for autistic individuals, addressing challenges such as the 'services cliff,' transition planning, and emotional impacts of changing structures, with a focus on the Indian context.
