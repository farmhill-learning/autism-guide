Navigating education and schooling can present unique challenges and opportunities for autistic individuals, particularly during the teenage years. This collection of resources is designed to provide valuable insights and practical strategies for families, educators, and caregivers to support autistic students in their educational journey.

These resources cover a wide array of topics, including understanding the educational needs of autistic students, effective teaching methods, accommodations and supports, social skills development, and preparing for transitions during adolescence. Each resource is thoughtfully curated to offer evidence-based information and actionable advice to foster a positive and inclusive learning environment for autistic teenagers.
