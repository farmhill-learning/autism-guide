Understanding autism is the first step in creating a more inclusive and supportive environment for individuals on the autism spectrum.This collection provides essential information for families, educators, and professionals beginning their journey to understand autism.

These resources cover fundamental concepts about autism spectrum disorder, how to recognize early signs, and evidence-based information about causes and interventions. A short history of how the understanding of autism has evolved is also presented. Each resource is carefully created to provide accurate, up-to-date information aligned with current research and best practices.
