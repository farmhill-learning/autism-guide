This collection of resources offers lesson plans and educational strategies specifically designed for educators teaching students with autism. These lesson plans cover various topics, including social skills, financial literacy, and more, to support the unique learning needs of students on the autism spectrum. By implementing these tailored lesson plans, educators can create an inclusive and effective learning environment that fosters growth and development for all students.
